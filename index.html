Trackmyholidays.com will allow you to easily see how much holiday leave time you have available

To start:

  1. Configure holiday information for your company in the "Dates" tab above
  2. Configure localisation settings in the "View" tab
  3. Close the settings window and select the first and last date for each holiday you are planning
  4. See how many days you have left to plan in the top right corner
  5. To return to the settings window at any point, click the spanner in the top left corner
